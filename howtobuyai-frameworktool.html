Step 1: Define Your Use Case

Start by clearly stating the primary business problem you want to solve with AI. This will be the title of your framework.

Step 2: Apply The Golden Rules

Rule 1: Define Your Problem in Dollars

Quantify the specific, measurable cost of this problem. This is the anchor for your entire ROI calculation.

Rule 2: Align the Price to Your Outcome

Ensure the vendor's pricing scales with the value you receive. Choose the model that best fits your problem.

Rule 3: Demand Guardrails to Control Costs

Implement contractual safeguards to provide budget predictability and prevent bill shock.
